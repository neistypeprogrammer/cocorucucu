Good morning everyone. First, I’d like to give you all my compli-ments on being hired at Mason Bank. My name is John Peters, and it is my job to conduct your orientation
Because you all will become a part of our communications staff, it is important that you forge relationships with everyone here.
Accordingly, I’m first going to introduce you all to people from all of our various divisions.
After that, we will spend the afternoon going through the employment manual.
Before you leave, the technical support will be providing you with your individual passwords so you can access your company accounts by tomorrow.
If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to approach me. Now let’s begin.


모두들 좋은 아침입니다. 먼저, Mason Bank에 고용되신 것에 대해 제 찬사를 보냅니다.
John Peters이며 당신의 오리엔테이션을 담당하는 일을 맡고 있습니다.
당신들은 모두 우리의 정보통신 직원이 될 것이기 때문에 이곳에 있는 모두와 관계를 구축하는 것이 중요합니다.
따라서, 저는 먼저 우리의 모든 부서에서 오신 분들을 여러분께 소개하겠습니다.
